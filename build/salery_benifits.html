Hometown as payroll foreman

How does your workplace strengthen its employees' work-life balance?

As a forward-thinking employer, you understand the importance of supporting your team both in and outside of work. Offering home cleaning as a payroll benefit enhances employees' work-life balance, reducing the strain of household chores so they can focus on their well-being and productivity.

How can you as an employer help?

Putting the everyday puzzle together can be something of an art. There are leftovers from kindergarten, training, cooking and finally the horrendous cleaning. Then performing at work can easily cause it to spill over. As an employer, it is difficult to help with all the needs that employees have privately, but there are ways to create more work-life balance for your employees.

But what is work-life balance really? In short, it is an expression that describes the balance between work and private life. The more of this balance there is - the better you both feel and perform, at work and privately.

Exercise and personal health are some important parts for a richer life outside of work, and there many companies are already starting with benefits such as wellness allowances. Something that is often forgotten in this equation is the home and the time spent on it, for things like cleaning. By offering house cleaning as a salary benefit, you as an employer can remove an irritating piece of the everyday puzzle for your employees and, as a bonus, also increase their performance during working hours. Win-win simply!

Questions and Answers

How does it work for the employer in practical terms?

Instead of having the employee apply for a tax reduction in the return, an "extra salary" is paid to the employee and then a net salary deduction is deducted for the cleaning. The invoice is assigned to the employee but sent to the employer.

How do employees order homestay as a benefit?

Get in touch with your employer and then contact us together to book homestay.

How much housework can you get as a benefit?

The total labor cost must be at least SEK 1,000 and at most SEK 100,000.

Can you get a calculation example?

Of course! The employee's salary statement will then look like below:

Salary SEK 30,000

Extra salary SEK 1,000

Total salary SEK 31,000

Tax on SEK 31,000 SEK 10,000

Net salary deduction- SEK 1,000

Total paidSEK 20,000
