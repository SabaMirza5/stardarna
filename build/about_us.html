About Städarna

The Beginning of a Journey

In 2008, Städarna was founded with a vision to revolutionize the cleaning industry. Today, we are one of Sweden’s largest home service companies, active in 24 locations and helping thousands of customers each month.

Our Journey

From an Idea to Reality

The journey started with Tjoffe Sjögren, who was inspired to create a cleaning company that people could trust. Over time, Städarna grew from a small team into a company with over 800 employees, all united by a shared goal.

Our Vision

People-Centered Cleaning Services

At Städarna, we prioritize people — both our employees and our customers. Our vision is to have the industry’s most satisfied customers and proudest employees, while changing the perception of the cleaning industry.

Our Core values

Our core values—Responsibility, Treatment, and Quality—drive everything we do. With a commitment to satisfied customers and proud employees, we ensure exceptional service by taking ownership, offering world-class hospitality, and maintaining the highest quality standards.

Employee Safety Comes First

While customer satisfaction is vital, we never compromise on the safety and well-being of our employees. Everyone deserves to feel 100% secure in their work environment.

In the past, we've turned down projects where we couldn’t guarantee this, and we’ll do it again, no matter how big the client. Our commitment to safety is unwavering.

Why Choose Städarna?

Satisfied Customer Guarantee

If you’re not happy with our work, we’ll return to make it right. Just report your concern promptly, and we’ll take care of the rest.

Confidentiality Assured

Our employees are bound by confidentiality agreements, ensuring your privacy at both home and office.

Comprehensive Liability Insurance

Your home and belongings are fully protected by our insurance policies.

Fair Working Conditions

We provide a collective agreement for our employees, ensuring a better work environment. When our employees thrive, your home benefits too.

Criminal Record Screening

For your peace of mind, all our employees undergo criminal record checks before entering your home.

Consistent Quality

The same team will handle your home and office cleaning, ensuring consistent, top-tier service.

Eco-Friendly Practices

We use environmentally conscious cleaning products, safe for both your home and the planet.
