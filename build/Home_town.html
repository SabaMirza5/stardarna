No preparation

If you don't want to do any special preparation to reset your home, it's good to keep in mind that it may take a couple of cleaning sessions before the home is completely clean. We only recommend you not choosing an introductory cleaning service if you are switching directly from another cleaning company.

Initial cleaning ( recommended )

For those who want to get a good result from the first cleaning, we recommend an initial cleaning. Here, we carry out the same stages as during regular house cleaning, but set aside more hours to be able to guarantee a really clean result from the first occasion.

Major cleaning

For those who have high demands on a fresh home or a very dirty home, the usual steps in a home cleaning are not always sufficient to give a clean feeling. For those customers who have not had cleaning help before, we recommend starting with a major cleaning.
