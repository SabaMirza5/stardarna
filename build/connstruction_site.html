Cleaning after house renovation

Have you renovated or built a new one? It is extremely important to remove all dust particles that collect during construction to ensure a good indoor environment. When the construction work is finished, it is completely understandable that you want to quickly unpack the moving boxes and get started with everyday life again. We carry out a thorough construction cleaning to get your home sparkling clean and free of dust particles that could otherwise affect health. 

How much does a construction cleaning cost?

The cost of a construction cleaning depends on the size of the object. Contact our friendly customer service and we will book a free meeting where we introduce ourselves and go over your needs.
